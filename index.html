  • Youth project & volunteer work registration

Silver
Individual: 500K–1M UGX
Institution: 2M–5M UGX
  • Eco-friendly welcome kit & certificates

Gold
Individual: 1.5M–2M UGX
Institution: 6M–10M UGX
  • All Silver benefits
  • Visibility in conservation projects
  • Exclusive networking events
  • Priority event invitations
  • Quarterly impact report
Platinum
Individual: 2.5M–4.5M UGX
Institution: 20M–40M UGX
  • All Gold benefits
  • Co-branding on project materials
  • Strategic partnership access
  • Named in annual report
  • Private ED briefing
Diamond
Individual: 5M+ UGX
Institution: 50M+ UGX
  • All Platinum benefits
  • Named conservation project/corridor
  • Board-level engagement
  • Bespoke ESG reporting package
  • Media & PR recognition
  • Annual CEO briefing
  • Exposure visits to project sites
